Prevention from Corona Virus

Stay home, Stay Safe

how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

regularly clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces like phones, doorknobs, light switches and countertops

maintain distance

watch your distance, at least 1 meter from others. avoid going on unnecessary trips

don't touch face

don't touch eyes, nose or mouth with unwashed hands. Always remember to keep your hands clean

wash your hand

Wash your hands frequently using soap and water or an alcohol-based hand rub

use napkin

cover mouth and nose with flexed elbow or tissue when coughing or sneezing. dispose of used tissue immediately

wear a mask

always wear a mask while going out and wear your mask properly
